Deploying Arcee models on Amazon Bedrock

In this video, you will learn how to deploy Arcee Small Language Models on Amazon Bedrock.

First, we look at the Arcee models available in Bedrock. Then, we deploy two models: SuperNova, a high-performance commercial model based on LLama-3.1–70b, and SuperNova Lite, a smaller variant based on Llama-3.1–8b. We test the models in the Bedrock playground and with the Bedrock API.
