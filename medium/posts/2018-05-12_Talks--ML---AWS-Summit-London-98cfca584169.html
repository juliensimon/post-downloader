Talks: ML @ AWS Summit London

The AWS Summit London just took place and I had to please to take part in three 20-minute Machine Learning sessions on Twitch. You’ll find the videos below. Happy viewing!

An overview of Machine Learning on AWS

I’m joined by my colleagues Denis Batalov (Principal Solutions Architect for Machine Learning, EMEA) and Paul Armstrong (Principal Solution Architect, UK) to discuss what ML is, what services are available on AWS and what kind of problems they can help you solve. A nice recap if you need to catch up on our latest services!

Machine Learning at scale with Amazon SageMaker

I’m joined by two special guests from Hotels.com: Matt Fryer( Chief Data Science Officer) and Adrian Woodhead (Principal Engineer). They explain how they use ML to solve complex problems at scale and also tell us about the AWS services involved. Then, Denis is back with a cool SageMaker demo on churn prediction.

Using Machine Learning for Gaming

I’m joined by Peter Chapman (Solutions Architect for Gaming, UK) and Steve Turner (Enterprise Solutions Architect, UK). We discuss how ML can help companies simplify game development and game operations.
