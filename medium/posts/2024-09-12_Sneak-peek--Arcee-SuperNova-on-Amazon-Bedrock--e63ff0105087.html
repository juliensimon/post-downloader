Sneak peek: Arcee SuperNova on Amazon Bedrock!

In this video, you will get a sneak peek at Arcee-SuperNova, a new 70B model built by Arcee.ai, running as a custom model import on Amazon Bedrock!

Arcee SuperNova excels in instruction following and human preference scores, outperforming Llama 70B-Instruct, as well as Llama 405B, Claude Sonnet 3.5, and GPT-4o in many general benchmarks.

If your organization is interested in running Arcee SuperNova on Amazon Bedrock, please get in touch, and let’s make it happen.
