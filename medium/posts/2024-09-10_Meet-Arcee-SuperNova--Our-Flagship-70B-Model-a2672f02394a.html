Meet Arcee-SuperNova: Our Flagship 70B Model

Today, Arcee.ai AI is happy to launch SuperNova, a 70 billion parameter language model designed for enterprise deployment, featuring advanced instruction-following capabilities and full customization options.

The model aims to provide a powerful, ownable alternative to API-based services from OpenAI and Anthropic, addressing key concerns around data privacy, model stability, and customization.
