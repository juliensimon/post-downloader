Tech talk: How Synapse Medicine leverages Hugging Face to improve medication safety

During this webinar, Cynthia Périer, Data Scientist at Synapse Medicine and myself discuss how Synapse Medicine leverages Hugging Face to improve medication safety.

The French Agency for Digital Health wanted to provide accurate medication information to Sante.fr users. They reached out to Synapse Medicine to integrate its medication chatbot, named Galien, on the Sante.fr website. Synapse Medicine used Hugging Face machine learning models during the chatbot development. Listen and learn :)
