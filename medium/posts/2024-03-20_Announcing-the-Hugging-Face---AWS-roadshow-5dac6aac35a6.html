Announcing the Hugging Face / AWS roadshow

US friends, the Hugging Face / Amazon Web Services (AWS) roadshow is three weeks away.

If you’re an AWS customer and would like to schedule a 1:1 meeting with me, please reach out to your AWS Account Manager, or send an email to amer-hugging-face-roadshow-support-team@amazon.com

  • Seattle, WA — April 9–11
  • San Francisco, CA — April 12
  • Santa Clara, CA — April 15–16
  • Los Angeles, CA — April 18–19
  • Boston, MA — April 22–23
  • New York City, NY — April 24–26
  • Austin, TX — May 28–31
  • Arlington, Washington DC — June 3–5

We will be hosting customer workshops in Seattle, Santa Clara, NYC, and Austin on these dates, and AWS customers can use the email address above to register.

I will also speak at the following public events:

  • AI Accelerator Institute Generative AI Summit, April 16, San Jose
  • Open Data Science Conference (ODSC) East, April 23, Boston
  • Machine Learning Week 2024, June 6, Phoenix

By the way, if you’re a meetup organizer, I’m open to doing meetups in the evening. Please contact me and let’s try to make it happen.
