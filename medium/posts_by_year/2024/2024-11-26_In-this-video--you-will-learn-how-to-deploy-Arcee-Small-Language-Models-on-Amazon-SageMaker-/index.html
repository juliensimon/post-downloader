Video: Deploying Arcee AI models in minutes with Amazon SageMaker JumpStart

In this video, you will learn how to deploy Arcee Small Language Models on Amazon SageMaker JumpStart. This is by far the simplest way to deploy Arcee models on AWS.

First, we deploy Arcee Nova, a powerful 72-billion parameter model, on a multi-GPU instance using a sample notebook provided by Arcee. Then, we deploy Arcee Lite, a compact 1.5B model, in one click on a small cost-effective GPU instance.
